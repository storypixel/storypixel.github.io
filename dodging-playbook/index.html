General Info

  • For play calls we will call out targets based on a number. The person on the left side is 1, next to them is 2, and so on.
  • During nearly all calls, players who are not throwing should be pump-faking.
  • Corners are the players on the left- and right-most side of the court.
  • Listen for people saying ball left or ball right and send a ball to the corresponding corner.

Offensive Calls

  • 3 balls — We meet in the middle and designate one or two throwers on a specific target. watch →
  • 4 balls — We call insides or outsides on a number.
  • Insides — The two middle players throw at the called number. watch →
  • Outsides — Both corners throw at the called number. watch →

Defensive Calls

  • Home — The designated player throws from their current position at the player of their choosing. This is a pre-counter, meaning you release the ball before or as the other team throws. watch →
  • Away — All ball-holders charge the center line and the designated player throws at a target of their choosing (typically the player who just threw). watch →
  • Mirror — The player directly opposite the opposing player who threw has the green light to throw. watch →
  • Middle — If we are left with only one ball, it goes to a player in the middle whose job is to bait balls by taking an offensive position. They should almost never throw that last ball. watch →
