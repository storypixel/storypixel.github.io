General Info

  • For play calls we will call out targets based on a number. The person on the left side is 1, next to them is 2 and so on.
  • During nearly all calls players who are not throwing should be pump faking.
  • Corners are the players on the left and right most side of the court.
  • Listen for people saying ball left or ball right and send a ball to the corresponding corner.

3 Ball Offensive Plays Calls

  • Huddle — Meet in the middle of the court at the back line and decide which of the three players with balls should throw. (Typically Josh and Daniel will be playcalling if we are on the court).
  • If a corner throws a ball, the player in the middle should shift slightly towards that side and backwards to protect the corner as they backpedal. As a general rule, you should not pass the corner the balls until they are at or near the back line. Wait until the other team is not in an active/ready position to pass the ball. The player holding the ball should remain active as a corner would.
  • Almost always should maintain two balls.
  • Non-throwing players should be pump faking.

4 Ball Offensive Throws:

  • Huddle — (the following are options to be called during the huddle)
  • Single ball throws — we huddle in the middle and designate one player to throw a single ball. We should always try to maintain 3 balls for defense but this is not a hard and fast rule if you see a good opportunity.
  • This should be called when we are trying to burn time on the clock or are down players
  • Kill left/right — The two people on the left or right throw balls are the same target. This will sound like kill left on 3 which means the left two players holding balls on our side will aim at the third player from the left on the opposing team.
  • Insides — The two players in the middle will pick a target. This will sound like insides on 5 which means the middle two players holding balls will aim at the 5th person from the left on the opposing team.

5 or 6 Ball Calls

  • Same calls as 4 balls if we have 4 or more players. Aka huddle and discuss target. We can designate 3 throwers on a single target in this situation.

2 or 3 Ball Defensive Calls

  • Each corner should have a ball in their possession if feasible.
  • Red light — do not throw. hold ball but actively work to push the other team back and keep them off balance with pump fakes.
  • Green light — The corner designated as having green light has the opportunity to counter the opposing team’s attack. They do not have to throw if there is not a good opportunity.
  • Only one corner should have green light at a time
  • Home/Away (typically only 3 ball call):
  • Home — The player holding a ball opposite the opposing player who just threw should throw at them from the backline or near where they were standing when the other player released.
  • Away — All players holding balls should rush the center line. The player holding a ball opposite the opposing player who just threw should throw at them from at or near the center line.
  • Crash (2 ball call) — If we are up players and the other team only has 1 or 2 remaining. After the opposing team throws the two corners holding the balls rush and both attack. If there is more than one player they should target the player who just threw.

1 Ball Defensive Call

  • Middle — Pass the ball to the player in the middle. They should take an offensive position and try to bait the other team to throw balls at them so we can regain ball control. This is not a required play, just an option for those who are very confident in their blocking ability or just feeling spicy. The ball can also be sent to a corner and they will take an offensive position to pump fake.

Miscellaneous Calls

  • Speed ball — throw quickly and don’t worry too much about ball control. This can be done near the end of games where we are down or just for fun.
  • Have fun — do whatever the fuck you want just try not to give up all the balls pls
  • Pitch back — On the opening rush one of the rushers will quickly toss a ball back to a designated player and they have option to target any player they want on the opposing team. This can also be done as a double pitch back where one of the rushers tosses both balls to two designated players and one of those two will release.

Rule Sets:

  • TPSL adjustments to main rules:
  • Games are not 3 minutes long then roll into no block, instead there will be a 22 minute game of regular play followed by 3 minutes of no blocking.
  • There will be 10-ish players on the court
  • We can shag but only out the backline with a hand raised.
