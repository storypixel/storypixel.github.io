Support

Having trouble with Calcuweighter? Here are some common solutions:

  • Camera not recognizing text? Make sure the whiteboard is well-lit and the text is clearly visible. Hold steady for best results.
  • Voice input not working? Ensure microphone permissions are enabled in Settings > Calcuweighter > Microphone.
  • PRs not saving? Check that you have available storage on your device. PRs are stored locally.
  • App crashing? Try deleting and reinstalling the app. Your PRs will be preserved if you have iCloud backup enabled.
