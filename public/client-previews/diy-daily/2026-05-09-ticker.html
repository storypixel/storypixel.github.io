Q Update

Today futures are higher with the Nasdaq leading the way up over 2%. On tap is the FOMC press release and we already got jobless claims that came in at the lowest level since mid-May. Over the last few years a tight jobs market like this has meant the Fed is in no rush to cut rates and that has usually been a negative for stocks. This morning however folks seem to be shrugging it off and bidding tech up hard ahead of the Fed. Watch the press release closely since a dovish read despite the strong jobs picture is what this rally is banking on.
